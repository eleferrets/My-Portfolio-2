Project Overview

Freedom is a small platformer about a struggle. It features cats, dinosaurs, scary peas (vegetables are scary), special effects, and many other things not necessary to the story! A fun feature is being able to pet both Cats and Dogs (and maybe even llamas)!?! I was working on this game for the better part of 3 years, and it is finally done.

I hope you'll enjoy it! I can assure you it will be free and compatible with your computer, regardless of the operating system.
